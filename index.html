Presentation

Hoankiemair project in a tangible and interactive simulation to evaluate the impact of pedestrian areas

birth of the project and main problem

Pollution is something that is becoming very worrying nowadays because of its harmful effects for the planet as well as for us.

The main problem is that the pollution is caused in part by the traffic of cars and motorcycles in the city.

Project

The project was carried out by a group of scientific researchers in order to perform different simulations to observe different data such as pollution, different areas with a lot of traffics.

Simulation of the HoakiemairProject.

  • Graphic of the extended pedestrian zone

    The visualisation is at 7 am

    We observe on this graph that from 7 o'clock in the morning, a high rate of car and motorcycle traffic in the city as we observe in each type of simulation (e.g. the one where there was no pedestrian zone and the one that is applied at the moment). With the help of the pedestrian zone that has been extended, the zone where there is no risk of health problems has been extended as well, but the problem is that if this zone is extended, the traffic will not decrease but rather increase in some places that did not have much traffic. We observe through the AQI range explanation that some areas are in purple, this means that it can affect quite severely the health of everyone.some areas that were in yellow have become red and this means that healthy people will be noticeably affected.people with breathing or heart problems will experience reduced endurance in activities.these individuals and elders should remain indoors and restrict activities.The reason is that many people have to take the car and motorcycle to go to work .

  • Graphic of the extended pedestrian zone

    The visualisation is at 12 am

    We observe on this graph that around 12 hours, the rate of traffics is slightly decreasing as we observe in each type of simulation (example where there was no pedestrian zone and the one that is applied at this time). The problem is that if this zone is extended, traffics will not go down but will increase in some places that did not have much traffics. We observe through the AQI range explanation that some areas are in purple, but these areas are much smaller than at 7 a.m. Some areas that were in yellow have become red.Only these red areas have also become smaller than at 7 a.m. We now observe mostly yellow areas that advise the few hypersensitive induividuals should reduce outdoor exercise.
