Presentation

Hoankiemair project in a tangible and interactive simulation to evaluate the impact of pedestrian areas

birth of the project

Pollution is something that is becoming very worrying nowadays because of its harmful effects for the planet as well as for us. Le problème est que la pollution est causé en partie par le traffique de motos et de voitures.So, the objective would be to reduce the pollution without having too many consequences on the road traffic and without creating high risk areas for pedestrians.
